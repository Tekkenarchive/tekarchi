Street Fighter X Tekken is a crossover fighting game developed by Capcom, bringing together characters from both the Street Fighter and Tekken franchises. The game uses a 2D fighting system similar to Street Fighter, combined with tag-team mechanics that allow players to switch between two characters during battle. It features a large roster including iconic fighters like Ryu, Ken, Kazuya, and Nina. The game also introduced the Gem System, which provides gameplay bonuses and customization options. It stands out as a unique blend of two legendary fighting styles.
