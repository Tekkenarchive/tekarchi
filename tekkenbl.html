Description

Tekken: Bloodline (2022) is an anime series based on the Tekken franchise, focusing on the story of Jin Kazama. After a tragic event involving his mother, Jun Kazama, Jin is taken in and trained by his grandfather, Heihachi Mishima. As he grows stronger, Jin enters the King of Iron Fist Tournament to seek revenge and confront the dark power within him. The series closely adapts elements from Tekken 3 while featuring iconic characters such as Kazuya Mishima, Paul Phoenix, and King, combining intense fight scenes with a more faithful narrative to the games.
