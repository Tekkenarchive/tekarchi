Tekken: The Motion Picture (1998) is an anime film based on the early Tekken games. It follows Jun Kazama as she investigates the mysterious Mishima Corporation and crosses paths with Kazuya Mishima during the King of Iron Fist Tournament. The story blends elements from Tekken 1 and Tekken 2, featuring intense battles, supernatural themes, and the dark legacy of the Mishima family. With classic animation style and a focus on character-driven storytelling, it offers a unique take on the Tekken universe.
