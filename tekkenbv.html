Description

Tekken: Blood Vengeance (2011) is a fully CGI-animated film based on the Tekken franchise. The story centers on Ling Xiaoyu, who is sent on a mission by Anna Williams to infiltrate an international school and investigate a mysterious student named Shin Kamiya. Along the way, she teams up with Alisa Bosconovitch, leading to a deeper conspiracy involving the Mishima family. Featuring iconic characters like Kazuya Mishima and Heihachi Mishima, the film blends high-quality animation with intense fight sequences and a storyline closely tied to the events of Tekken 5 and Tekken 6.
