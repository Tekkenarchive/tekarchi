Description

Tekken 2: Kazuya's Revenge (2014) is a live-action film that serves as a prequel to the 2009 Tekken movie. The story follows a young man who wakes up with no memory of his past and is taken in by a mysterious crime organization. As he is trained to become a ruthless assassin, he slowly discovers his true identity as Kazuya Mishima. The film explores his transformation into one of the franchise’s most iconic antagonists, diving into themes of control, identity, and revenge. While it differs from the original game storyline, it offers a darker and more grounded take on Kazuya’s origins.
